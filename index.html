Our Services

Function Halls

Stage 1 (A/C) and Stage 2 (Non-A/C) function halls are fully furnished and equipped, available at affordable prices, and offer complete facilities for your events.

Accomodation

Four fully furnished rooms with A/C, attached washrooms, and 24/7 hot water. Each room accommodates 4-6 guests, featuring a queen-sized bed, Wi-Fi, and power backup.

Convention Hall

Perfect for corporate meetings, nuclear family events, birthdays, and special gatherings.

24 X 7 Power and Water Facility

24/7 power backup with generators and bore wells ensures constant electricity and water.

Parking facility

Ample parking space for 5-6 cars, bikes, and other vehicles.

Solar Powered Hot Water Facility

Solar-powered hot water system guarantees uninterrupted hot water supply.
